Our Story

NH Motorsports is a student-run organisation based at New Horizon Institute of Technology and Management of Mumbai University. Comprising of a variety of students of different branches ranging from second year to third year, the team designs, constructs, and races an open-wheel car in the world’s largest engineering design competition, Formula SAE. The competition, consisting of both static and dynamic events, is the largest university competition in the world with over 500 teams worldwide. Starting to compete since this year, 2019, the team believes in steadily improving its performance over the next few years. The beginning of this team has been a result of a mutual culture of strong fundamental engineering understanding, innovative concept developments and a passion to perform at our highest level.


Our Belief

We, at NH Motorsports, believe that participation and showcasing our skills & technological knowledge is of atmost importance. The greatest sense of achievement comes with knowing that we are giving our best and utilising our full potential as a team.

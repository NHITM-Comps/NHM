Welcome to NH Motorsports

We are a team of students primarily from the New horizon Institute of Technology and Management who design, build, and race a formula-style car. Comprising of young engineers from various cultural and academic backgrounds, our team comes together every year to construct the best race car we can: a truly cutting-edge piece of automotive engineering! After the design and assembly process, we participate in prestigious international competitions with other university teams from around the India. We invite you to explore our website to learn about our history, look through our galleries, and contact our representative.

MANAGEMENT

Management of the formula student team and the design process was found to be essential to the efficient completion of any complex engineering project.


SUCCESS

One step closer to building the cars and engineers of tomorrow.

QUALITY ENGINEERING

All students on our team specializes in their own tasks. The ability to use one's time effectively or productively, especially at work and gain experience with their time on the team


PASSION

It's a never ending battle of making your cars better and also trying to be better yourself. Our race cars fuel our motivation to work even harder.

THE JOB
DESIGN, BUILD, RACE, AND WIN!

There are many different jobs that need to be done within the team, both engineering and logistical. The tasks our members have to perform range from contacting sponsors to constructing 3D models of the car to manufacturing the components to organising the logistics for the competitions and press events! Our team employs a highly diverse group of students with various technical and cultural backgrounds to ensure that the team is firing on all cylinders and spinning all four wheels throughout the entirety of the season!

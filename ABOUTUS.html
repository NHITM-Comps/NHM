A few words about us

NH Motorsport is a student-run activity based at New Horizon Institude of Technology and Management, Thane.This includes students of different branch from second year to final year engineering This team design,constructs, & races an open-wheel car in the world's largest engineering design competition i.e. Formula Bharat. This competition consists of both static & dynamic events and it is largest university competition in world with over 500 teams worldwide. Competing since 2019, the team has steadity improved in its performance. This has been result of a culture of strong fundamental engineering understanding, innovative concept developement & a passion to perform at our highest level. by participating in nNH Motorsports, students gain invaluable partical skills in their respective fields of both engineering & business.

Our College

College provides many outlets for NH Motorsports to thrive. The College generously enables students to utilize machine shop, computer lab and a work shop. The college supports us by sharing many resources and facilities with the team. To give the students the possibility to work beyond the opening hours of main building, there is a room in a main building just for the racing team. This room is the center of the team acitivities and assembling area of the racecar. In addition to that, the team is allowed to use a laboratory, seminar rooms, computer rooms and other laboratories.
